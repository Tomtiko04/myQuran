About Quran.mo

The Noble Quran is the central religious text of islam. Muslims
believe the Quran is the book of divine guidance and direction
of mankind, and consider the original Arabic text the final
revelation of Allah.

All translations of the originalArabic text are thus interpretations
of the original meanings and should be embraced as such.
